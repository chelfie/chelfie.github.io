How to use

Step by step

Take your Chelfie

When you open the Chelfie app, a camera will pop up where you can take your selfie. Just pose, capture your outfit, and you're done! Check out the features on the right to add text and color to amp up your picture.

Share.

Your Chelfie deserves to be seen by more than just your camera so share it! You can send it directly to a friend, a group, or your followers. Get feedback, showcase your personal style, and share your pictures with the world.

Shop.

The Chelfie app isn't just a place to share selfies, you can shop too! Click the button on the bottom right of the camera screen to go to your feed with both Chelfies from your friends and other recommended products. Now you and your friends can shop together even if you're miles apart!

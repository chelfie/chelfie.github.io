App

How do I get started?

First, download the Chelfie app in the app store or on google play. Then go ahead and create an account so you can start exploring the features!

How do I use the app?

Chelfie is fun and simple to use! Once you’ve created your account, you can start exploring the app to see it’s different features. For a step by step tutorial on how to use Chelfie click here.

Shopping on Solebrity

Can I shop on Chelfie?

See something you like? You can shop on Chelfie too! On the top left of your main feed screen you’ll see a button that will show you similar products to the item in the picture.

What are my payment options?

Our partner brands and retailers accept all major credit and debit cards such as VISA, MasterCard, and American Express.

Will my order be delivered to me?

Items are delivered directly to you from our partner brands and retailers. To view an estimated time of delivery for a product, please check your confirmation email and go to the product page for the item. As soon as your order ships, you will receive an email containing a tracking number from our partner brand or retailer. We are very selective on our partners and strive to quickly deliver the items to our customers. Some items (i.e. from partners like TomTop, AliExpress) can take up to 5 weeks because the items are shipped internationally.

Can I trust the products on the app?

We partner only with trusted retailers and brands and maintain a very high level of quality assurance. If you have any doubt of the authenticity of your product, please contact us immediately.

Can I earn on Chelfie?

The answer is Yes!

Profile and Account

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

When you are on the login screen of Chelfie click forgot password and it’ll redirect you to our reset password screen.

How do I change my username, email or password?

To change your username, email address, or password, tap on your picture or Edit Profile in your Settings. You will receive a confirmation of the change.

Is my profile public?

Yes it is, but you can also use the app privately through our direct messaging feature to share your chelfies exclusively with your friends.

How do I change my notifications?

To edit your notifications, tap Notifications in your Settings.

Orders

How do I cancel an order?

Please refer to your order confirmation email. Our partner brands and retailers cannot promise changes or cancellations to orders that have already begun processing but will always strive to accommodate your requests. If you’re still having an issue, please contact us.

How do I track my order?

To check the status of your order, refer to your order confirmation email and go to Order History and locate your order number.

Shipping

How do I track my shipment?

To check the status of your shipment, refer to your order confirmation email and go to Order History and locate your tracking number.

How do I resolve a shipping issue?

Please refer to your order confirmation email and contact the brand or retailer directly. If you’re still having an issue, please contact us.

Returns

Can I return an item I bought?

Our partner brands and retailers will accept returns for most items within 30 days of purchase, granted that the items are unused and in their original packaging. You will receive credit for the item subtotal to your credit or debit card as soon as your items are processed. Shipping and taxes may not be included. Please note that our brands and retailers cannot accept returns on final-sale merchandise, damaged items, and items marked non-returnable or non-exchangeable.

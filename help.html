App

How do I get started?

First, download the Chelfie app in the app store or on google play. Then go ahead and create an account so you can start exploring the features!

How do I use the app?

  1. Start at the login screen. Click on the sign up button to create an account. You can either sign up with your email or link the app directly through facebook.
  2. After you sign up, the app will take you directly to the home screen.
  3. Home screen: Anytime you open the app it will direct you right to the camera. Tap the larger circle icon to capture an image and you will then be able to unleash your creativity.
    • Tap the pencil icon to write text.
    • Tap the three circle icon to doodle to your hearts content.
    • Tap the scissors icon to edit your picture by cropping it or rotating the image.
    • Tap the button that says Save so your photo will save onto your camera roll
  4. Sending a Chelfie:
    • Tap the pencil icon to write text.
    • Tap the three circle icon to doodle to your hearts content.
    • Tap the scissors icon to edit your picture by cropping it or rotating the image.
    • Tap the button that says Save so your photo will save onto your camera roll
  5. Tap the checkmark icon to add it to your looks.
  6. Tap the Share to… button to share it with your friends on social media.
  7. To send a private message scroll down and tap their name or find them in the search bar at the top.
  8. Adding your friends:
    • Take a chelfie
    • Tap the send button
    • Tap the circular icon in the top right hand corner
    • On this screen, you can either invite your friends by tapping on the Invite button in the top right hand corner or you can type their name in the search bar at the top and click the follow button
  9. Shop with Friends
    • Tap the ShopWFriends icon to take you to your direct messages.
    • You are automatically presented your received messages where you can tap a friend's name to view their message.
    • Swipe left to dislike and right to like.
    • Tap the Shop the Look button to view where they purchased it or a similar item
    • Tap on the sent button to view the number of likes and dislikes for the messages you already sent.
  10. Shop
    • Tap the Shop icon on the bottom right corner of the home screen
      • Swipe a picture to right, to like it
      • Swipe a picture to the left to dislike it
    • When you find something you like, tap on the Shop The Look button which is located at the top of your screen. Our advanced AI technology will show you products that are either similar or exact, to those in the picture
      • Tap the Buy button to be redirected to the retailer's website where you can purchase the item
      • Tap the circular button with a question mark in it to send the item externally
      • Tap the circular button with a heart in it to like the item

Shopping on Chelfie

What brands are featured on Chelfie?

For an extensive list, click here to see who we partnered with to bring you the latest and most popular clothing items.

What are my payment options?

Our partner brands and retailers accept all major credit and debit cards such as VISA, MasterCard, and American Express.

How will my order be delivered to me?

Items are delivered directly to you from our partner brands and retailers. To view an estimated time of delivery for a product, please check your confirmation email and go to the product page for the item. As soon as your order ships, you will receive an email containing a tracking number from our partner brand or retailer. We are very selective on our partners and strive to quickly deliver the items to our customers. Some items (i.e. from partners like TomTop, AliExpress) can take up to 5 weeks because the items are shipped internationally.

Can I trust the products on the app?

We partner only with trusted retailers and brands and maintain a very high level of quality assurance. If you have any doubt of the authenticity of your product, please contact us immediately.

Can I earn rewards on Chelfie?

Yes! We share 10% of our earned revenue from each product you endorse that results in a purchase by one of your followers

Cash out: Once you’ve reached $20 in your account, you can cash out by tapping the button and sending the money to your paypal account. More cash out options will be forthcoming

Badges: The more items your followers purchase, the higher your badge level, qualifying you for VIP treatments, free items, and other rewards (on top of the cash you already earn from Chelfie). You can find the badge level under your profile.

Profile and Account

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

When you are on the login screen of Chelfie click forgot password and it’ll redirect you to our reset password screen.

How do I change my username, email or password?

To change your username, email address, or password, tap on your picture or Edit Profile in your Settings. You will receive a confirmation of the change.

Is my profile public?

Yes it is, but you can also use the app privately through our direct messaging feature to share your chelfies exclusively with your friends.

How do I change my notifications?

To edit your notifications, tap Notifications in your Settings.

Orders

How do I cancel an order?

Please refer to your order confirmation email. Our partner brands and retailers cannot promise changes or cancellations to orders that have already begun processing but will always strive to accommodate your requests. If you’re still having an issue, please contact us.

How do I track my order?

To check the status of your order, refer to your order confirmation email and go to Order History and locate your order number.

Shipping

How do I track my shipment?

To check the status of your shipment, refer to your order confirmation email and go to Order History and locate your tracking number.

How do I resolve a shipping issue?

Please refer to your order confirmation email and contact the brand or retailer directly. If you’re still having an issue, please contact us.

Returns

Can I return an item I bought?

Our partner brands and retailers will accept returns for most items within 30 days of purchase, granted that the items are unused and in their original packaging. You will receive credit for the item subtotal to your credit or debit card as soon as your items are processed. Shipping and taxes may not be included. Please note that our brands and retailers cannot accept returns on final-sale merchandise, damaged items, and items marked non-returnable or non-exchangeable.

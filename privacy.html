Note: This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 1, 2016.
Solebrity, Inc. is fully committed to protecting the privacy of our users. Our Privacy Policy sets out how we collect, use, and share information that identifies you (or associated with you) and the choices you have.
By setting up an account with us or using and accessing the Solebrity reservation system, website, and mobile app (the “Services”), you consent to the use of your Personal Information as described in this Privacy Policy.

What personal information does Solebrity collect?

The information we gather from our users enables us to personalize and enhance our services, improve the quality of our user experience, and allows our users to set up accounts that can be used to interact with other users and merchants. We collect the following types of information:
Personal Information including but not limited to your name, username, phone number, email address, zip code, and your friends’ email addresses (which we will only use to invite them to use the Services).
Information you supply regarding your personal preferences and interests (you may choose not to provide us with any or all of the information we request, however, this will hinder your ability to take advantage of some or all of our features).
Information you supply through your social network providers (“SNP”) Facebook and Twitter if you decide to create your account via these sites (i.e. your "real" name, email address, listings created, comments and reviews posted, and other pertinent information you make publicly available via the social networks). The information extracted may depend on the privacy settings you have with your social network account as well as credentials you provide us to link your Solebrity account with these “Third Party” social network services. If you provide your Third Party social network account credentials to us, you understand some content and/or information in those accounts ("Third Party Account Information") may be transmitted into your account with us if you authorize such transmissions, and that Third Party Account Information transmitted to our services is covered by this Privacy Policy.
Non-Identifying Information that you provide as part of registration and the administration and personalization of your profile on Solebrity’s website and mobile application.

How does Solebrity use cookies?

Solebrity’s site, application, services use "cookies" to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that is sent to your mobile device or personal computer for record-keeping. We use these cookies to save your login information for future logins to Solebrity services and enable certain features of the Solebrity services to better understand your interactions with our application and website and to monitor usage and Solebrity website and application traffic to ensure optimal user performance. When you log off from Solebrity’s website or application and close your browser, the session cookies are deleted from your personal computer and mobile device. You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Solebrity services.

How you can delete or change your information?

All Solebrity users may review, update, correct or delete their personal information in their account by contacting us or by editing such profile via the website and mobile applications. We use commercially reasonable efforts to honor your request. We may retain an archived copy of your information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes. You can always opt not to disclose information. If you do not wish to receive email or other mail from us, please email us at privacy@solebrity.me.

How we share your information?

We may provide third parties, third party websites of our partner networks, advertisers, partner merchants, retailers and affiliates with aggregate statistical information and analytics about users of our services.

How your information is transferred?

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers outside of your country or other governmental jurisdiction. If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, Solebrity transfers personal information to the United States and processes it there. Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to the transfer.

How we handle information security?

We may make legally required disclosures of any breach of information security, confidentiality, or integrity of your unencrypted electronically stored personal information to ensure the integrity of our services and systems.

What is our policy towards children?

Our services are only available to persons aged 13 and over.
